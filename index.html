Welcome to my website

I am a PhD candidate as well as a passionate informatician at the L3S Research Center at the Leibniz Universität Hannover. My research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Explainable AI(XAI). Specifically, my work focuses on developing methods for explaining behaviors of machine learning-/deep learning-based decision systems (black-boxes).

My research is supported by the State Ministry of Science and Culture of Lower Saxony, within the PhD Program "Responsible Artificial Intelligence in the Digital Society".
